Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardary
To, Ms. Soha Parvez
The Back Bedroom
28, D.D.Puram
Bareilly, India.
Dear Ms. Parvez,
We are pleased to inform that you have been accepted to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardary. Students shalls be reqiured to the report to the Chamber of Reception upon arrival. We await your owl no later than 31 July.
Please make yout way to Kings Cross Station onto platform nine three-quaters where you will meet the Hogwarts Express. Term begins 1 September.
